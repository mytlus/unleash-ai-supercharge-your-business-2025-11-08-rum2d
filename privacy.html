Cookie Policy

A cookie is a small file that resides on your computer's hard drive that allows us to recognize you as a returning user and to personalize your experience at our site. We may use cookies to collect, store, and track information for statistical purposes to operate our site and services.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the operation of our website and services
  • Analytical Cookies: Used to understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Functional Cookies: Used to remember your preferences and personalize your experience
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to track visitors across websites and display targeted advertising

Cookie Management

You can choose to have your computer warn you each time a cookie is being sent, or you can choose to turn off all cookies via your browser settings. Like most websites, if you turn your cookies off, some of our services may not function properly. However, you can still place orders over the telephone by contacting customer service.

We may allow third-party service providers to place cookies on your device for analytics, advertising, and other purposes. These third parties have their own privacy policies governing their use of cookies. We do not control these cookies and encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third parties.

We may use tracking technologies such as pixels, web beacons, and similar technologies to track your activity on our website and across other websites. These technologies help us understand your browsing habits, improve our services, and deliver targeted content and advertising.
