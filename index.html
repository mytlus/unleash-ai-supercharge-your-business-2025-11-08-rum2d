About Twenty One AI

Founded in 2020 by a team of AI researchers and enterprise software veterans, Twenty One AI emerged from a simple vision: to democratize artificial intelligence for businesses of all sizes. Our founders recognized that most companies lacked access to sophisticated AI tools due to prohibitive costs and complexity. We built Twenty One AI to change that narrative, creating an intuitive platform that delivers enterprise-grade AI capabilities without the enterprise-grade price tag. In just four years, we've grown from a scrappy startup to a trusted partner for over 2,000 companies worldwide, processing millions of transactions daily and generating billions in value for our customers.

Our mission is unwavering: to empower every organization with intelligent automation that drives growth, efficiency, and innovation. We believe that AI should be accessible, ethical, and transformative. We're committed to continuous innovation, maintaining the highest security standards, and building long-term partnerships with our customers. Our core values—transparency, excellence, and customer success—guide every decision we make. We invest heavily in research and development to stay at the forefront of AI technology, and we're passionate about helping our customers navigate their digital transformation journey with confidence and success.
